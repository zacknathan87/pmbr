Malaysia Bid Round

Malaysia Bid Round (MBR)

Malaysia Bid Round is an annual bid exercise organised by PETRONAS which offers potential investors with different opportunities as well as study opportunities to grow their energy portfolio and create value.

Explore Opportunities on Offer in Malaysia

Malaysia Bid Round Plus (MBR+)

The Malaysia Bid Round Plus (MBR+) is an initiative by PETRONAS that offers an additional avenue for investors to explore and acquire Discovered Resource Opportunities (DRO) and Late Life Assets (LLA) in Malaysia. This program complements the annual Malaysia Bid Round (MBR) by providing more opportunities for investment beyond the regular bidding rounds. MBR+ is designed to attract a diverse range of investors, including oil and gas companies, solution partners, researchers, and academia, by offering access to valuable exploration and production data through the PETRONAS myPROdata platform.
